Electrical Safety Inspections

Complete diagnostic inspections designed to identify hazards before they cause expensive property damage.

Protect Your Property with a Thorough Inspection

Hidden electrical faults are some of the most dangerous and expensive issues a property owner can face. Our comprehensive safety inspections uncover these problems early.

We uncover hidden electrical hazards before they become dangerous and expensive problems.

Whether you're buying a new home, renovating a commercial space, or just doing routine maintenance, we provide itemized diagnostic reports to help you make informed decisions.

  • Code Compliance Verification
  • Load and Capacity Testing
  • Fire Hazard Identification
  • Grounding System Checks

What does an electrical inspection cover?

▼

We inspect panels, outlets, switches, ground fault circuits, and perform load testing to ensure your system isn't overloaded.

How often should I get an inspection?

▼

We recommend a full inspection every 3-5 years, or whenever you are buying, selling, or significantly renovating a property.

Do you provide a written report?

▼

Yes, you will receive a fully itemized written report detailing our findings, which can be useful for insurance or real estate transactions.
