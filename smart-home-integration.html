Smart Home Panel Integration

Take control of your home's energy consumption with app-connected smart electrical panels and load managers.

The Future of Home Electrical Systems

Traditional breaker boxes are a thing of the past. Smart electrical panels allow you to monitor, control, and optimize your home's energy consumption right from your phone.

Track consumption in real-time and automatically shift high-demand loads to off-peak hours for massive energy savings.

If you are planning to add solar panels, battery backups, or an EV charger, a smart panel acts as the brain that balances power availability seamlessly across all systems.

  • EV Charger Load Management
  • Solar & Battery Integration
  • Real-time Usage Tracking
  • App-based Circuit Controls

What is a smart electrical panel?

▼

It's an internet-connected replacement for your traditional breaker box that allows you to individually monitor and switch off circuits via a mobile app.

Can you install EV chargers alongside this?

▼

Yes. By using smart load balancing, we can often install Level 2 EV chargers without needing a highly expensive utility service line upgrade.
